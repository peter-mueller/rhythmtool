Log in as a user

  1. Log in with your username and password and click the login button.

  2. Now you are on the home page.

Failed log in as a user

  1. Log in with your username and password and click the login button.

  2. A toast appears, if the credentials were wrong.

Create a rhythm by text

A rhythm represents a sequential arrangement of hits and pauses. This creates a beat. It has no defined meter, so the interpration for playing must be done by the user. For example a rhythm with length 12 can be seen as a 3/4 beat with sixteenth notes or a 12/8 beat with eight notes.

Recipe:

  1. Log in with your username and password and click the login button.

  2. Click the circled plus button in the bottom right corner.

  3. Select the random option from the radio button group and click the continue button.
